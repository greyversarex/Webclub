WEBCOREX Hero – Digital Core (Art Direction)

We are NOT creating a decorative 3D object.

We are creating the visual symbol of Webcorex.

This object will become the company's identity and the first thing every visitor remembers.

Its purpose is to communicate one idea:

"All digital products are born from one intelligent core."

The object must feel premium, elegant and futuristic, not flashy or cyberpunk.

Main Concept

Create an abstract "Digital Core".

In the center there is a floating crystal energy core.

Around it there are several thin transparent glass rings.

The rings rotate slowly on different axes.

Small glowing particles travel along the rings like streams of digital data.

Everything should feel alive, calm and expensive.

No aggressive animation.

No gaming aesthetics.

No sci-fi spaceship.

Imagine an object that Apple would place inside an exhibition room.

The Crystal

The center crystal is the heart of the composition.

Requirements:

• Transparent crystal material
• Multi-layer geometry
• Refraction
• Reflection
• Internal light scattering
• Slight blue/purple energy glow
• Very subtle pulsating light
• Perfectly sharp edges

The crystal should look valuable.

Almost like a rare technological diamond.

Glass Rings

Use 4–6 rings.

Different diameters.

Different tilt angles.

Ultra-thin premium glass.

Some rings are complete circles.

Some are slightly broken or segmented.

They rotate extremely slowly.

Each ring catches light differently.

The rings should create depth instead of visual noise.

Data Particles

Tiny glowing particles move along ring paths.

Movement is smooth.

Very slow.

Particles leave almost invisible light trails.

This creates the feeling that information is constantly flowing through the system.

Floating Technology Nodes

Around the object place only a few tiny floating symbols.

AI

CRM

ERP

WEB

Mobile

Automation

Do NOT make them labels around the object.

Instead they should feel like tiny satellites.

Minimal.

Elegant.

Semi-transparent.

They should never steal attention from the core.

Lighting

Lighting is everything.

Avoid bright bloom.

Avoid overexposed white light.

The object should be readable from every angle.

Use:

HDRI reflections

Soft rim light

Blue key light

Purple secondary light

Subtle ambient lighting

Glass must remain visible at all times.

Materials

Everything must use physically based rendering.

Glass should have:

Transmission

Thickness

IOR

Subtle chromatic aberration

High-quality reflections

Clean edges

Absolutely no cheap plastic appearance.

Interaction

Auto rotation.

Very slow.

Mouse movement slightly changes viewing angle.

Dragging rotates the entire object.

After release, smooth inertia returns.

No sudden movement.

No dramatic effects.

Background

Deep dark blue gradient.

Almost black.

Very subtle fog.

Very few floating particles.

No stars.

No planets.

No galaxies.

No colorful explosions.

The background exists only to emphasize the object.

Platform

The object should NOT float randomly.

Place it on a premium circular glass platform.

Almost invisible.

Very soft reflection.

This makes the object feel like an expensive exhibition piece.

Emotional Goal

When someone opens the website, they should immediately think:

"This company builds serious technology."

Not:

"Nice 3D animation."

The object must represent intelligence, engineering, precision and innovation.

Less decoration.

More meaning.

Premium over flashy.

Apple over Cyberpunk.

Luxury over spectacle.
